Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

MegaTech IT Solutions is committed to providing high-quality services, digital products, and educational resources. Please read this Refund Policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. Digital Products

Due to the nature of digital products, all sales of downloadable files, templates, software, eBooks, guides, and similar digital resources are generally final once the product has been delivered or made available for download.

2. Online Courses

Enrollment fees for online courses are generally non-refundable once access has been granted. If you experience technical issues preventing access to your course, we will work with you to resolve the problem promptly.

3. Website Development Services

Refund requests for website design or development services are evaluated based on the stage of the project.

  • If work has not yet started, a refund may be considered.
  • If the project is already in progress, only the unused portion of the service may be eligible for a partial refund, where applicable.
  • Completed projects are generally not eligible for refunds.

4. Computer Repair and Remote IT Support

Payments for completed repair services, remote support sessions, software installation, troubleshooting, or consulting are generally non-refundable once the service has been successfully delivered.

If a service cannot be completed due to circumstances within our control, we may offer a partial or full refund at our discretion.

5. Duplicate Payments

If you accidentally make duplicate payments for the same product or service, please contact us as soon as possible. Verified duplicate payments will be refunded using the original payment method whenever possible.

6. Subscription Services

If we offer subscription-based services, cancellations will stop future billing but will not normally result in refunds for billing periods that have already begun unless required by applicable law.

7. Exceptions

Refunds may be considered in exceptional situations, including:

  • Duplicate transactions.
  • Failure to deliver a purchased product or service.
  • Billing errors.
  • Other circumstances determined by MegaTech IT Solutions after review.

8. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact MegaTech IT Solutions with:

  • Your full name.
  • Your email address.
  • Proof of payment.
  • The product or service purchased.
  • A detailed explanation of your request.

Each request will be reviewed individually, and we will respond as soon as reasonably possible.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any updates become effective immediately after being published on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Refund Policy, please contact MegaTech IT Solutions using the contact information available on our website.
